FAQ

Everything you need to know.

Is there a free trial? +
Yes. You can try WA Bulk Sender for free with up to 10 contacts before committing to a purchase. No credit card required. Just reach out on WhatsApp and we will set you up with trial access instantly.
Is WA Bulk Sender safe to use? +
Yes. WA Bulk Sender uses a smart delay system that mimics human sending patterns. You can configure minimum and maximum delays between messages from the Settings tab. This significantly reduces the risk of triggering spam detection. Always send to opted-in contacts and start conservatively on new numbers.
Does it use the WhatsApp Business API? +
No. WA Bulk Sender works entirely through WhatsApp Web in your Chrome browser. No API keys, no Meta Business Account, no per-message fees. It works with your existing personal or business WhatsApp number at a flat annual cost.
What is the Google Sheet template and how does it work? +
When you purchase, you get a pre-formatted Google Sheet template with columns for phone, name, brand, product, coupon, link and more. Just fill in your contacts, export the sheet as a CSV, and upload it into WA Bulk Sender. No spreadsheet expertise required at all.
Can I personalize messages for each contact? +
Absolutely. Use any column from your CSV as a variable by wrapping it in curly braces. Supported out of the box: name, brand, product, coupon, link and more. Every message sent is completely unique, making each recipient feel it was written personally for them.
Will my WhatsApp number get banned? +
WA Bulk Sender's randomized delay logic is specifically designed to minimize this risk. We recommend messaging only opted-in contacts, starting with smaller batches on new numbers, and using the daily limit setting. No tool can provide a 100% guarantee, but our delay system is a significant protective measure.
How many contacts can I message at once? +
There is no hard cap imposed by WA Bulk Sender. You can upload as many contacts as needed. We recommend using the built-in daily limit (e.g. 200 per day) to maintain a natural sending rhythm for your number.
